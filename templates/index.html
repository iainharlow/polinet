Poli-Net

Going beyond two-party descriptions of politicians.

Here you can select a political figure from the list below.

Hint: The list includes most candidates for federal office (House, Senate, Presidency) from the 2014 election cycle, and is therefore quite long (>2000 names). The list is arranged alphabetically by first name (as commonly used, e.g. Ted Cruz appears under 'Ted' not 'Edward'). With the dropdown menu active, you can jump to a particular politician by typing the first letters of their first name.

The displays below will update with your selected politician. You can see:

  • The 'cluster' the politician you chose best fits into, based on their sources of funding
  • How their income distribution compares to the average: Which sectors do they recieve more of their support from?
  • Biographical links, including contact details. Make your own voice heard!
